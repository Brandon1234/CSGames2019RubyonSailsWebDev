Public Service Commission of Canada

The Public Service Commission (PSC) is responsible for promoting and safeguarding a merit-based, representative and non-partisan public service that serves all Canadians, in collaboration with its stakeholders. It also manages the tools for public service recruitment, providing applicants and managers with a single portal to access all public service job opportunities. The PSC is a federal institution that is part of the Intergovernmental Affairs, Northern Affairs and Internal Trade portfolio and it reports independently to Parliament on its mandate.
